Our Services

What HVAC & Plumbing Services Are Available?

Comprehensive home services backed by our done-right guarantee. Every job includes upfront pricing before we start.

HVAC Services

Keep your home comfortable year-round with our expert heating and cooling solutions. From emergency repairs to complete system installations, our licensed HVAC technicians deliver reliable results.

Austin summers regularly push temperatures above 100°F, making a dependable air conditioning system essential. Our HVAC team specializes in all major brands — including Trane, Carrier, Lennox, and Rheem — and every technician holds current EPA 608 certification for refrigerant handling. Whether you need a quick capacitor swap or a full 16-SEER system upgrade, we size every installation using Manual J load calculations to ensure maximum efficiency and even comfort throughout your home.

AC Repair & Replacement

Fast diagnostics and repair for all major brands. Full system replacement with energy-efficient options. Our technicians carry the most common parts on their trucks so most repairs are completed in a single visit. When replacement is the better option, we help you choose the right SEER-rated unit for your home size and budget, and handle permits and installation from start to finish.

Heating & Furnace

Furnace repair, installation, and heat pump services. Stay warm with reliable heating solutions. We service gas furnaces, electric furnaces, and dual-fuel heat pump systems. Our team performs thorough safety inspections — including carbon monoxide testing and heat exchanger checks — to keep your family safe during Austin's occasional cold snaps.

Maintenance Plans

Seasonal tune-ups starting at $49. Prevent breakdowns and extend system life with regular maintenance. Each tune-up includes a comprehensive 21-point inspection covering refrigerant levels, electrical connections, thermostat calibration, condensate drain clearing, and airflow testing. Regular maintenance can reduce energy bills by up to 15% and extend your system's lifespan by several years.

Duct Cleaning & IAQ

Professional duct cleaning, air quality testing, and filtration upgrades for a healthier home. Over time, dust, allergens, pet dander, and mold spores build up inside your ductwork. Our team uses high-powered vacuum equipment and rotary brush systems to remove contaminants, and we can install HEPA filters, UV germicidal lights, or whole-home air purifiers for ongoing protection.

Smart Thermostats

Expert installation of Nest, Ecobee, and other smart thermostats. Save energy and control comfort remotely. A smart thermostat can reduce heating and cooling costs by 10–23% per year by learning your schedule and adjusting temperatures automatically. We verify compatibility with your existing HVAC system, handle all wiring, and walk you through the app setup before we leave.

Plumbing Services

From minor leaks to major installations, our certified plumbers provide lasting solutions. We handle residential plumbing with the same professionalism and transparency as every BrightPath service.

Plumbing problems rarely happen at a convenient time. That's why our licensed plumbers are available for same-day appointments and 24/7 emergency calls. We arrive with fully stocked trucks, use video camera inspections to diagnose issues accurately the first time, and explain every option before starting work. From a dripping faucet to a complete re-pipe, BrightPath treats your home with care and delivers repairs that last.

Plumbing Repairs

Leaks, clogs, toilets, faucets, and pipe repair. Quick diagnosis and lasting fixes for any plumbing issue. We repair and replace all types of pipes, including copper, PEX, and PVC. Our plumbers use leak detection equipment to locate hidden leaks behind walls or under slabs without unnecessary demolition, saving you time and money.

Water Heaters

Tank and tankless water heater repair, replacement, and installation. Energy-efficient options available. We service all major brands including Rheem, AO Smith, Bradford White, and Rinnai. Tankless water heaters can reduce energy usage by up to 34% compared to traditional tank units. We help you choose the right capacity for your household and handle all code-compliant installation.

Drain Cleaning

Professional drain clearing and hydro-jetting for stubborn clogs. Camera inspections available. Slow drains, gurgling sounds, and recurring backups are signs of a deeper blockage. Our hydro-jetting service uses high-pressure water to clear grease, roots, and debris from your pipes without harsh chemicals. We include a post-cleaning camera inspection so you can see the results.

Sewer Line Service

Sewer line inspections, repair, and replacement using advanced camera and trenchless technology. Trenchless pipe repair — also called pipe lining or pipe bursting — lets us fix damaged sewer lines without digging up your yard, driveway, or landscaping. We start with a video inspection to assess the damage and recommend the most cost-effective repair method.
